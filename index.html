Volume Rendering for Hand Anatomy
Feb. 2022 - Present (Advised by Prof. Jernej Barbiƒç)

Research on volume rendering for musculoskeletal system. We developed a volume renderer based on volume ray-casting. It considers both the hand MRI volume data, and the geometry of its inner structures as known segmentation, permitting fine control over the appearance of each tissue using customized transfer functions. More to come!

Ben-Day Dots Shading
Jan. 2022
code / shadertoy

Ben-Day dots was a technique developed in the late 19th century to print colors onto the newspapers. Given an input image, this program simulates the physical process of printing and applies a similar screen space effect to the image.

CPU Ray Tracer
Apr. 2020

A ray tracer capable of rendering opaque surface (triangles and spheres) with lighting, shadows, and reflection. Anti-aliasing and soft shadows were implemented to improve the image quality. BVH and OpenMP were utilized to improve the performance. Toon shading is for fun.

Jell-O
Feb. 2021
code

Jell-O is a physically-based simulation of a jello cube inside a cornell-box force field. It's modeled with a mass-spring system, capable of handling collisions, and rendered with OpenGL.
